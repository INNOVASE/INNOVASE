Design and implementation of an ML model for PdM 4.0 using NDT.

General context

The Engineer’s level Internships provide the opportunity for hands-on experience in a student affairs setting. I had the chance to add an interesting experience to my CV by doing a 5-month internship with OCP MS as part of OCP S.A. In this internship I worked on the following subject “Design and implementation of an ML model for PdM 4.0 using NDT.” in the next section I’ll explain to you the subject in more detail.

Internship subject

OCP Maintenance Solutions is positioned as a champion of Industry 4.0 in Morocco, it is an entity of the OCP group, having evolved within the group and acquired an expertise in the predictive maintenance, and having for strategic orientation the development of digital solutions digital solutions in the field of maintenance 4.0. OCP-MS's predictive maintenance project started in 2018 in collaboration with MIT. The project consists of the prediction of defect classes and their causes as well as the prediction of the residual remaining life using vibration data.
OCP Maintenance Solutions has an IoT platform called I-sense, this platform platform includes an Analytics module where predictive maintenance models have been implemented for prediction of defect classes and their causes as well as the prediction of the residual life of equipment using using vibration data. The objective of this internship is to make an extension of this extension of this module by developing new dedicated solutions for predictive maintenance using non-destructive testing techniques NDT.

Results of our project are :
Benchmark of NDT techniques and technical choice
Definition of the problematic and the functionalities to be developed
Collection and processing of data
Development and implementation of the model to solve the chosen problem
Interfacing with a database
Creation of web pages to visualize the results.
